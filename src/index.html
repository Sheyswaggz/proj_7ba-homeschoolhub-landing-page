Our Comprehensive Curriculum

Flexible, engaging, and comprehensive learning programs designed for K-12 students with personalized paths that adapt to each child's unique learning style and pace

K-5

Elementary Program

Building strong foundations in core subjects through hands-on learning and exploration. Ages 5-11 years.

  • Language Arts - Phonics & Reading
  • Mathematics - Hands-on Learning
  • Science - Experiments & Nature Study
  • Social Studies - Community & Culture
  • Arts & Enrichment Activities

Program Highlights:

  • Phonics-based reading instruction with living books
  • Hands-on math manipulatives for concrete understanding
  • Weekly science experiments and nature studies
  • Creative arts integration across all subjects
  • Social-emotional learning and character development

Weekly Hours: 17 hours of structured learning with flexible scheduling

6-8

Middle School Program

Developing critical thinking and independent learning skills across all disciplines. Ages 11-14 years.

  • Language Arts - Advanced Reading & Writing
  • Mathematics - Pre-Algebra & Algebra I
  • Science - Laboratory Experiences
  • Social Studies - World & American History
  • Electives - Technology, Arts & More

Program Highlights:

  • Project-based learning approach for real-world application
  • Pre-algebra and Algebra I preparation with mastery focus
  • Laboratory science experiences in life, physical, and earth sciences
  • Research and writing skills development
  • Technology integration and digital literacy

Weekly Hours: 21 hours of structured learning with increased independence

9-12

High School Program

College-preparatory curriculum with advanced coursework and real-world applications. Ages 14-18 years.

  • English - Literature & Composition
  • Mathematics - Algebra II through Calculus
  • Science - Biology, Chemistry, Physics (AP)
  • Social Studies - History & Government (AP)
  • Electives - Foreign Language & More

Program Highlights:

  • College-prep and honors courses with rigorous standards
  • Advanced Placement (AP) options in multiple subjects
  • Dual enrollment opportunities with local colleges
  • Career and college counseling services
  • Portfolio development and transcript preparation

Weekly Hours: 25 hours of structured learning with college-level rigor
